# Documentation

## Collection

<img width="1917" height="1027" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ca1ac37b-b9e3-4117-9be6-d948507ea479" />

Here you can browse the entire card catalog and see how many copies of each card you own using the small indicators displayed beneath the cards.

You need to synchronize the application with the game's data. Please note that the game appears to update the local collection file only when it starts. If you want to synchronize your collection, make sure to do so after restarting the game.

### Navigation

- **Left-click** a card to view the Agent's **Immortalized** version.
- **Right-click** a card to open a **detailed card view**.

## Statistics

<img width="1911" height="1027" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/fbe242f7-36c8-4ae8-9bde-8bb91f971af9" />

Here you can track the progress of your collection.

### Overall
Represents all cards in the game, grouped by rarity.

- **x1** represents unique cards. For example, **29 / 72 Common** means there are 72 Common cards in the game and you own 29 of them.
- **x3** represents unique cards for which you own all 3 copies.
- **All** represents the total number of cards owned, including duplicates.

### Sets
Represents all cards and their copies for each set.

### Syndicates
Represents all cards and their copies for each faction. This can help you see which faction you own the most cards from and guide your deck-building choices.

## Decks

<img width="1916" height="369" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4e1e693a-d153-4dda-aafa-578cfd7885c1" />

Here you can create a new deck or import one using a deck code provided by the game.  
The context menu on each deck allows you to **Clone**, **Rename**, **Export**, or **Delete** the deck.  
You can also mark decks as **Favorites**, which will place them at the top of the list.  
The deck list can be reorganized using **drag and drop**.

## Deckbuilder

<img width="1918" height="1029" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/269bb21b-3d71-445d-96e4-72e89dcad274" />

The first step when creating a deck is to add your two Divers. The catalog will only display Divers until both have been selected. Once your two Divers are chosen, the catalog will only show cards from Syndicates that are valid for that Divers combination.

### Adding / Removing Cards
You can use the **"+"** and **"-"** buttons directly in the catalog, but you can also left-click or right-click the card quantity displayed in the deck list.

### Card Availability in the Deck List
If your collection contains enough copies of a card for the quantity used in your deck, the card quantity will be displayed in green in the deck list. If you do not own enough copies, it will be displayed in red.

For more detailed information, hover your mouse over the card and look at the small indicators. The number of red indicators shows how many copies of that card are missing from your collection.

### Missing Cards Summary

<img width="337" height="97" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/efd2b024-98f0-45f7-b070-a8e626f4cce8" />

You can also check the top of your deck list, where you'll find a summary of your missing cards.

It displays:
- The **total number of missing cards**
- The **number of missing cards by rarity**

This is useful for quickly determining how many **Wildcards** you will need to craft the deck.


## Invalid cards

<img width="337" height="274" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d82d7b68-3ed7-420c-8277-bf317328e857" />

If a card is no longer allowed in the deck due to a change of Divers and, consequently, the list of allowed Syndicates, the card's name will be displayed in red to indicate that your deck is currently invalid because of that card.

**You cannot save or export an invalid deck**
